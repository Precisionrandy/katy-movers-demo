Your rights when moving in Texas

Only use a licensed mover

The TxDMV licenses moving companies and provides consumer protection so you can make a smart move. Texas law requires movers to give you a written estimate, a written contract before the move, and a brochure outlining your rights.

  • If a moving deal seems too good to be true, it probably is.
  • Never accept a verbal quote or agreement.
  • Never hire an unlicensed mover — Texas movers must be licensed by the TxDMV.
  • Read the contract before signing it.

More info at TxDMV.gov or call (888) 368-4689.
